dining table

This dining table was made out of European pine. The original inspiration were traditonal English-pub tables, which - in case of good weather - could have their legs easily dismantled and be easily rolled outside. The table had to be round in order to easily fit in the dining room. The main requirement was to make it larger enough in order to allow 4 people to dine comfortably, but with an option to fit extra 2 persons. The main feature are the legs: they are octagonal, tapered and glued-up to the table's bottom at 12-degrees angle. This allowed for stable construction and unique design. The finish was water lacquer.
