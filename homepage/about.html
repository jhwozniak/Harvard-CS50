About JJ Woodworks:

We are small family woodworking company based close to Warsaw, Poland. We specialize in custom woodworking projects, including unique free-standing pieces for individuals and complex solid-wood fit-outs for companies. Since establishment in 2016 we have finished dozens of custom pieces like tables, chairs, book cabinets, beds and mirror frames.

We work only with EU-certified solid wood like oak, pine, ash and walnut. Each wood piece is chosen together with a client. At JJ we usually use a mix of woodworking machinery and manual tools, with preferance to the latter. Our favorite finishes are natural oils and waxes. The furniture is made to last several generations, so you will not find cheap MDF, plastic connectors or toxic glue in our furniture. What you you will find however, will be such marks of high quality like dovetails and other exposed wooden joints, silky-smooth surfaces and eye-pleasing flow of wooden textures.
