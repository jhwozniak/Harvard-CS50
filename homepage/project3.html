pergola

This garden pergola has been our largest project so far. It is made out of European pine and it was installed in vicinity of Warsaw, Poland. We were inspiried by traditonal English-garden pergolas. The main difficulty was the relatively large terrace (4.5 x 3 metres). We wanted to keep the design simple and limited to 2 sqare columns only. Therefore, the overall contruction had to be stable enough in order to accomodate the weight and side-tensions. This was the reason for the use of wide beams, solid brackets and double rafters on both sides. We also decided to give rafters a traditonal "scalloped" ends. The whole project was finished with white-oak impregnate in order to minimize the impact of humidity.
